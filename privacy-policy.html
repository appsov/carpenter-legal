Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 3, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how the Carpenter mobile application (“Carpenter”, “App”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) collects, uses, and protects your data when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of data:

  • Device Information: such as device model, iOS version, screen size, and preferred language (used to optimize app support and layout).
  • Usage Data: anonymous statistics about feature usage, screen visits, and app performance (used to improve functionality).
  • User Messages: content of messages you send through our built-in support chat feature.
  • App Events: anonymized event logs for crash diagnostics and bug reporting.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To improve app features and user experience
  • To respond to your messages and support inquiries
  • To monitor app performance and fix bugs

3. Data Storage and Security

All data is securely stored using Firebase and Firestore services provided by Google. We apply reasonable technical measures to protect your information.

4. Tracking and Advertising

We do not use third-party advertising SDKs. If Apple’s App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt appears, it is used solely for analytics or marketing attribution purposes within Apple’s ecosystem.

5. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. Data may only be disclosed if required by law or legal process.

6. Children’s Privacy

This App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
